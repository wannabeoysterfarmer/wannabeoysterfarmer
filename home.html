Steven Chen

My photography interests revolve around railways, landscapes, and night scenes (especially when they

pertain to the Cyberpunk genre). Some of my recent recognitions include being selected as a Docent

Scholarship recipient by the Center for Railroad Photography & Art, Grand Prize and Third Place Winner

of the 2020 John E. Gruber Creative Photography Awards contest. A photographer of over five years, I

graduated from Emory University in 2020 and am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer

Science with the University of Pennsylvania.
