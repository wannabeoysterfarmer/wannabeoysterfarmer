Aerial Footage of Train #281 - Brookfield, WI

Six of Canadian Pacific's cherry-red locomotives haul a westbound freight train

through the sleepy town of Brookfield, WI on a winter afternoon. Footage was shot

with my DJI Mavic Mini drone. Music: Avicii - Waiting for Love // Date: 12/26/2020

 

 

Aerial Footage of T5 in Madison, WI

The regular evening freight train T5 rolls into the snow-covered city of Madison, WI. There

is something so surreal about being able to see the world from the sky. At night, the city

taking on a whole new light. Music: Alan Walker - Lost Control (Instrumental) //

Date: 12/17/2020.

 

 

Color Illustration - "Astro Kid"

After taking Scott Harris's illustration course on Udemy, I brushed up on my drawing

skills and created a few pieces while quarantined during the COVID-19 pandemic. "Astro

Kid" is a piece about hypothetical space travel in a dreamy, futuristic world.

Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Digital Illustration.

 

 

B&W Sketches - Layouts & Characters

Sketchings of both human figures and landscape design drawn from imagination. This

set of sketches depict a fictional small-town near the mountains along with fictional

characters. Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Digital Illustration, Realistic Sketching.

 

 

Realistic Renderings

Using Adobe Photoshop techniques, I am able to produce renderings from my

imagination. One of my favorite kinds of renderings is based on the phone and

train concept, inspired by my photographer friends from college. Attached

are a few of my favorites. Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom.

 

 

Drone Photography

The world often takes on a whole new perspective when viewed from above! So many

new angles to try out and so many places to explore. I use a DJI Mavic Mini and

shoot both photo and video. Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Photography, Videography.

Steven Chen "Choo-Choo"

I'm an explorer and adventurist at heart, with interests in digital media ranging from photography

to videography to illustration.

 

Most of what I know is self-taught through trial-and-error and supplemented through

online learning. Some of my recent recognitions include being selected as a 2020 Docent Scholarship

recipient by the Center for Railroad Photography & Art, and as the Grand Prize and Third Place Winner

of the 2020 John E. Gruber Creative Photography Awards contest (international).

 

With my academic background in chemistry, economics, and computer science, I tend to approach

my work through a highly precise and detailed lens. I graduated from Emory University in 2020 and

am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science with the University of Pennsylvania.
