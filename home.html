Aerial Footage of Westbound CP281 - Brookfield, WI

Six of Canadian Pacific's cherry-red locomotives haul a westbound freight train thru

the sleepy town of Brookfield, WI on a sunny afternoon. Footage was shot with my

DJI Mavic Mini drone.

Music: Avicii - Waiting for Love // Date: 12/26/2020

 

 

Aerial Footage of T5 in Madison, WI

The regular evening freight train T5 rolls into the snow-covered city of Madison, WI.

There is something so surreal about being able to view the world from above at night,

as if the city takes on a whole new light.

Music: Alan Walker - Lost Control (Instrumental) // Date: 12/17/2020.

 

 

Color Illustration - "Astro Kid"

After taking Scott Harris's illustration course on Udemy, I brushed up on my drawing

skills and created a few pieces while quarantined during the COVID-19 pandemic. "Astro

Kid" is a piece about hypothetical space travel in a dreamy, futuristic world.

Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Digital Illustration.

 

 

B&W Sketches - Layouts & Characters

These are sketchings of both human figures and landscape design drawn from imagination.

I've always enjoyed free-hand creation of figures and landscapes on a white background.

These scenes are based off a short story I wrote titled "As Long As It Takes" which

will be published on this website soon.

Techniques: Adobe Photoshop, Digital Illustration, Realistic Sketching.

Steven Chen "Choo-Choo"

I'm an explorer and adventurist at heart, with interests in digital media ranging from photography

to videography to illustration.

 

Most of what I know is self-taught through trial-and-error and supplemented through

online learning. Some of my recent recognitions include being selected as a 2020 Docent Scholarship

recipient by the Center for Railroad Photography & Art, and as the Grand Prize and Third Place Winner

of the 2020 John E. Gruber Creative Photography Awards contest (international).

 

With my academic background in chemistry, economics, and computer science, I tend to approach

my work through a highly precise and detailed lens. I graduated from Emory University in 2020 and

am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science with the University of Pennsylvania.
